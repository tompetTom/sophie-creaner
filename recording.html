Flutes

The flutes are silver and very famous. They come in some shapes and sizes, and make a certain sound. The different types are:

  • Flute
  • Alto flute
  • Piccolo

The flute (or silver flute, or concert flute) is the famous one. Alto flute very velvety indeed, Sophie's favourite really. The piccolo is high pitched and strong.

Sophie's favourite thing is working with writers to utilise her palette and inventing unique textures with novel instrument combinations...

Sophie isn't only limited to woodwinds! She loves to collaborate with colleagues in the industry to streamline your production experience – and deliver a perfected ensemble...

Process

Gear

Sophie workflow is simple – here's what you can expect:

  • Every project starts with a discussion of what you need and how Sophie can bring that to life.

  • Sophie then records some or all of the music and sends you an mp3 for revision.

  • Any necessary changes are made, and you get full quality .wav or .aiff files of the finished product.

  • The process usually takes between 24-72 hours for most projects.
