There are boundless possibilities for new sounds with woodwind instruments, especially when layering different lines together to create unique and exciting textures. Sophie records existing lines or can help to compose woodwind parts.

Sophie records all of these instruments:

  • Clarinets

    • Clarinet
    • Bass clarinet
    • Eb clarinet

  • Flutes

    • Flute
    • Alto flute
    • Piccolo

  • Recorders

    • Contrabass to sopranino
    • Renaissance, Medieval, Baroque, Modern

  • World Flutes

    • Irish flute
    • Bansuri (Indian)
    • Sáo trúc (Vietnamese)
    • Dizi (Chinese)

  • Saxophones

    • Soprano saxophone
    • Alto saxophone
    • Tenor saxophone
    • Baritone saxophone

She can also offer any combination of instruments by bringing in colleagues from the industry to collaborate with on a project. As well as getting fast results, this guarantees that all the parts will fit perfectly together – in tune and matching style.

Sophie's Career

Sophie performs primarily as a musician for West End shows, alongside being very active in the UK and European early music scenes. The best thing about West End shows is the variety of instruments and styles that she has to play on a daily basis, all to the highest professional standard. Sometimes, her musical theatre and early music branches colide, as with upcoming musical Treason, which has its first taste of performance in the Cadogan Hall this year, streamed online in March.

Lux Musicae

Sophie is a founding member of the mixed consort Lux Musicae London, formed to musically explore the transition from the Renaissance to the Early Baroque.
