Sophie is a woodwind multi-instrumentalist based in London, originally from Cork, Ireland. She records sessions, performs in theatre shows and plays early music.

Woodwind remote recording

Do you need woodwind tracks recorded for your project? Click below to see how Sophie could work with you.

Sophie records existing lines or can help to compose woodwind parts. There are boundless possibilities for new sounds with woodwind instruments, especially when layering different lines together to create unique and exciting textures.

She can record any of these instruments:

  • Clarinets

    • Clarinet
    • Bass clarinet
    • Eb clarinet

  • Flutes

    • Flute
    • Alto flute
    • Piccolo

  • Recorders

    • Contrabass to sopranino
    • Renaissance, Medieval, Baroque, Modern

  • World Flutes

    • Irish flute
    • Bansuri (Indian)
    • Sáo trúc (Vietnamese)
    • Dizi (Chinese)

  • Saxophones

    • Soprano saxophone
    • Alto saxophone
    • Tenor saxophone
    • Baritone saxophone

She can also offer any combination of instruments by bringing in colleagues from the industry to collaborate with on a project. This negates any disparities when recording musicians separately.
